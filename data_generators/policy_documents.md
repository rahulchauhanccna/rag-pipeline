# E-Commerce Customer Support Policies

## Shipping & Delivery Policies

### Standard Shipping
- **Delivery Time:** 5-7 business days
- **Cost:** $5.99 (FREE on orders over $50)
- **Carriers:** UPS, FedEx, USPS, DHL
- **Tracking:** Provided via email within 24 hours of shipment
- **Cut-off Time:** Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship same day

### Express Shipping
- **Delivery Time:** 2-3 business days
- **Cost:** $12.99
- **Guarantee:** On-time delivery or partial refund
- **Tracking:** Real-time updates every 4 hours

### Overnight Shipping
- **Delivery Time:** Next business day
- **Cost:** $24.99
- **Cut-off Time:** Orders must be placed before 11 AM EST
- **Guarantee:** Next-day delivery or full refund

### International Shipping
- **Delivery Time:** 7-14 business days
- **Cost:** Calculated at checkout based on weight and destination
- **Customs:** Customer responsible for duties and taxes
- **Tracking:** Provided for all international shipments

## Refund & Return Policies

### Standard Return Window
- **Timeframe:** 30 days from delivery date
- **Condition:** Items must be unused and in original packaging
- **Refund Method:** Original payment method
- **Processing Time:** 5-10 business days after we receive the item

### Extended Return Window (Premium Members)
- **Timeframe:** 60 days from delivery date
- **Condition:** Items must be in resalable condition
- **Refund Method:** Store credit or original payment method
- **Processing Time:** 3-5 business days

### Defective/Damaged Items
- **Timeframe:** Report within 7 days of delivery
- **Condition:** Any condition (defective or damaged in transit)
- **Refund Method:** Full refund or replacement at customer's choice
- **Processing Time:** 2-3 business days
- **Shipping:** FREE return shipping label provided

### Partial Refunds
- **Eligibility:** Items returned after 30 days but within 60 days
- **Refund Amount:** 80% of original price
- **Restocking Fee:** 20% applies
- **Processing Time:** 5-10 business days

## Delay & Compensation Policies

### Shipping Delays
- **Notification:** Email updates at 24, 48, and 72 hours
- **Compensation Threshold:** Delays exceeding 3 business days
- **Compensation Options:**
  - 10% store credit for 3-5 day delays
  - 20% store credit for 5-7 day delays
  - 25% store credit + free shipping on next order for 7+ day delays

### Weather-Related Delays
- **Policy:** No compensation for weather delays
- **Communication:** Proactive updates every 24 hours
- **Safety:** Carrier safety protocols take priority

### Carrier Delays
- **Our Responsibility:** Delays caused by our fulfillment process
- **Compensation:** Automatic store credit based on delay duration
- **Resolution:** We work directly with carrier for expedited delivery

## Order Cancellation Policies

### Before Shipment
- **Timeframe:** Up until order is marked "shipped"
- **Cancellation:** Full refund processed immediately
- **Notification:** Email confirmation within 1 hour

### After Shipment
- **Standard Orders:** Cannot be cancelled, must use return process
- **Express/Overnight:** May be intercepted for 50% fee (if carrier allows)
- **International:** Cannot be cancelled once in customs

## Special Circumstances

### Lost Packages
- **Investigation Period:** 7-10 business days
- **Replacement:** Shipped immediately if carrier confirms loss
- **Refund:** Full refund if customer prefers
- **Compensation:** 15% store credit for inconvenience

### Wrong Item Sent
- **Reporting:** Within 7 days of delivery
- **Resolution:** 
  - Keep wrong item (full refund + 20% store credit)
  - Return wrong item (free return shipping + replacement shipped immediately)
- **Processing:** 2-3 business days

### Late Delivery Compensation
- **Automatic:** Store credit applied to account
- **Manual Claim:** Contact support within 30 days of delivery
- **Verification:** Tracking information reviewed
- **Credit Amount:** Based on delay duration (see Delay & Compensation section)

- **Opt-in:** Required for promotional emails

## Escalation Procedures

### When to Escalate
- Compensation requests over $100
- Legal or regulatory concerns
- Repeated unresolved issues
- Media or public relations inquiries

### Escalation Path
1. **Level 1:** Customer Service Representative
2. **Level 2:** Senior Support Specialist
3. **Level 3:** Support Manager
4. **Level 4:** Director of Customer Experience

### Escalation Timeline
- **Level 1 → 2:** After 2 unsuccessful interactions
- **Level 2 → 3:** After 1 unsuccessful interaction
- **Level 3 → 4:** Immediate for high-value customers or legal issues

## Emergency Situations

### Product Recalls
- **Notification:** Email and phone within 24 hours
- **Return:** FREE return shipping, full refund
- **Replacement:** Available if customer prefers
- **Compensation:** 25% store credit for inconvenience

### Data Breaches
- **Notification:** Within 72 hours (legal requirement)
- **Credit Monitoring:** Free for 1 year
- **Identity Protection:** Provided at no cost
- **Support:** Dedicated hotline for affected customers

### System Outages
- **Communication:** Status page updated every 30 minutes
- **Compensation:** Service credits for affected customers
- **Extensions:** Order deadlines extended by outage duration
- **Support:** Priority handling for orders affected by outage
